General Chemistry


General Chemistry is the first chemistry course science and engineering majors will take in college. The following sections serve as an introduction to the fundamental concepts of chemistry. Topics covered include dimensional analysis, atomic structure, molecular bonding, chemical reactions, basic thermodynamics, and states of matter.
